Piano Lessons with John

Have you ever marveled at musicians that had excellent technique, mature musical understanding, and could play by ear? If so than this is your oppurtunity to become one of those musicians your self with my help!

What will my curriculum teach?

  • Basic chords & scales

    • Every great musician has a strong grasp of the fundamentals. Understanding complex chord structure, melody, and harmony requires a strong understanding of the basics.

  • Chord inversions

    • This grants flexibility with Chord voicings so you are not stuck playing basic root position voicings

  • Scale degree recognition

    • Being able to differentiate all diatonic chords within a scale is an essential component to playing by ear

  • Learning Music from multiple genres

    • The best way to learn to become musical and build your musical vocabulary is to study music and learn to play those songs

  • Chord progressions & voicings

    • These will aid in building musical vocabulary

  • Chord recognition ear training & much more!
